Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 1, 2026

Aeterna ("we", "our", or "us") is developed by Pgoby Solutions. This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Aeterna mobile application.

1. Data Collection and AI Processing

Aeterna is an AI-powered Digital Twin application. To function, the app processes text data provided by you (e.g., WhatsApp chat exports).

  • Data Processing: Your chat data is processed temporarily to train the AI persona.
  • AI Provider: We use Google Gemini API as our third-party AI provider. Data sent to the AI is anonymized where possible and is not used to train Google's public models.
  • No Permanent Storage: We do not store your chat logs on our servers permanently. Once the analysis is complete, the raw data is discarded.

2. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your data. We only share data with service providers necessary to operate the app:

  • Google Cloud / Gemini: For AI processing.
  • RevenueCat: For processing subscription payments (we do not see your credit card info).
